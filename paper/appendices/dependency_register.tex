\section{Proof-home and downstream consequence register}
\label{app:dependency-register}

\textbf{Register rule after Tier~3 hardening.} Sections~2--8 are the local proof home for the core theorem spine. Sections~9--11 classify, audit, or route consequences of that spine. Downstream application manuscripts remain consequence-facing only and do not function as live proof sources for the local foundational arc.
\end{fixedbox}

\begin{remark}
The register is intentionally asymmetric. Sections~2--8 may be cited as the local theorem spine of the manuscript. Sections~9--11 may classify or audit consequences of that spine, but by Theorem~\ref{thm:no-downstream-backflow} they do not function as new proof sources for it.
\end{remark}
